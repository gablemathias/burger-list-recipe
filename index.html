The Classic Burger Recipe

So I feel that you're feeling inspired to make a good burger in your home.
So, this recipe will bring to you the classic one! Forget about iFood or any other app and made it yourself! You'll not regret!

Total:30 mins Yield: Makes 4 servings

Ingredients

  • ½ kg ground lean beef
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt and pepper
  • ½ cup flour
  1. Preheat a grill for high heat and lightly oil grate;
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together egg, salt and pepper. Place ground beef and flour into the mixture. With hands or a fork, mix until well blended;
  3. Form into 4 or more patties;
  4. Place patties on the prepared grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
